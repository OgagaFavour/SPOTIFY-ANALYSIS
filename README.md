# Spotify User Engagement and Streaming Trends Analysis

## Business Context / Problem Statement  
Spotify is one of the world’s leading music streaming platforms, where understanding user behavior and content performance is critical for growth. Product managers, marketing teams, and data analysts need data-driven insights to make informed decisions about playlist curation, artist promotion, and user engagement strategies. This analysis aims to identify key factors influencing streaming trends and user engagement, enabling stakeholders to optimize platform performance and drive business growth.

---

## Project Objectives  
- Identify top-performing artists, tracks, and playlists based on streaming metrics  
- Analyze the impact of user engagement (e.g., skips, repeats, playlist additions) on overall streaming performance  
- Provide actionable recommendations for marketing, product, and content teams  

---

## Data Description  
- **Source:** Spotify streaming statistics (2020–2022)  
- **Period covered:** 2020–2021 and 2021–2022 listening data  
- **Key fields:** Track name, artist, album, genre, streams, skips, repeats, playlist adds, user demographics (age, region)  
- **Size of dataset:** 1M+ streams across thousands of tracks and playlists  

---

## Analytical Approach  
- Data cleaning and preprocessing using **Power BI**  
- Exploratory data analysis (EDA) using **Excel**  
- Interactive data visualization using **Power BI**  

---

## Key Insights & Findings  
- Top-performing tracks are those with high repeat rates and strong playlist placements  
- Genres with balanced representation across regions tend to drive higher engagement  
- User engagement metrics (skips vs. repeats) are strongly correlated with track longevity and playlist success  

---

## Impact on Business Decision-Making  
- Marketing teams can optimize promotional campaigns based on streaming efficiency metrics  
- Product managers can refine playlist curation strategies to maximize engagement  
- Spotify can make informed decisions about artist partnerships and content investments  
- **Influenced KPIs:** user retention, average streams per user, playlist engagement rate, revenue growth  

---

## Recommendations  
- Focus on promoting artists and tracks with high repeat and playlist-add rates  
- Invest in genre diversity to capture broader audience segments  
- Continuously monitor engagement metrics to inform playlist curation and marketing campaigns
